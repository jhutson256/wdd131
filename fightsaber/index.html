Welcome!

Jedi padawans and Sith apprentices alike, welcome to Fightsaber! Here you will find lore on all of the different parts of a lightsaber, and each part's different variations. Whether you are looking to construct your own lightsaber, or just interested in learning more about lightsabers, this is the website for you! This website also features a simple, but fun and interactive lightsaber duel you can take part in! As you browse the lore of lightsabers, you can change the background image to the one the force calls you to pick. You can pick between 4 different background images in the drop down menu below.
